U.S. President Donald Trump reacted to Robert Mueller's testimony on July 24, hours after Mueller bluntly dismissed Trump's claims of total exoneration in the federal probe of Russia’s 2016 election interference.Mueller told Congress he explicitly did not clear the president of obstructing his investigation. The former special counsel also rejected Trump’s assertions that the probe was a “witch hunt” and hoax.Trump also called the probe a "waste of time" and stated it was an embarrassment to the country.
