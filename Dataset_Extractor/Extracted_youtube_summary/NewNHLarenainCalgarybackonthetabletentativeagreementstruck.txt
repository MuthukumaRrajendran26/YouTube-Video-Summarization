Plans for a new arena in Calgary to replace the Saddledome are accelerating with a tentative agreement in place.The city, the Calgary Flames and the Calgary Stampede, which owns the land, have agreed in principle to terms on an event centre that would be the new home of the city’s NHL team.The deal was to be presented to council behind closed doors on the afternoon of July 22, and then put to a vote on whether to make it public immediately.
