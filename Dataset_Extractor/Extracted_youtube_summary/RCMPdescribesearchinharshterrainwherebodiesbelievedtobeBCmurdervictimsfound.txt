Manitoba RCMP spokesperson Sgt. Paul Manaigre spoke about the terrain where the bodies believed to be that of the two BC murder suspects, calling it very "harsh terrain" and said to reach that spot, "it's difficult to walk."He said during the search they did find one new item in the area that is part of the investigation, but could not comment further.
