RCMP say there have been additional sightings of two young men wanted in the deaths of three people in northern B.C.The police force said Thursday that Bryer Schmegelsky, 18, and Kam McLeod, 19, were seen twice in the Gillam, Man., area.The most recent sighting was on Monday, RCMP Cpl. Julie Courchaine told reporters.The sightings took place before a torched RAV 4 — which police say was used by the suspects — was discovered near the northern Manitoba community.There have been no new reports of stolen vehicles, Courchaine said.“At this point in the investigation, we believe they are still in the area,” said Courchaine.
