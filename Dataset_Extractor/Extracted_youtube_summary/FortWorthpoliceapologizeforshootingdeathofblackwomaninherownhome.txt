Fort Worth police chief Edwin Kraus held a brief news conference concerning the charges against former police officer Aaron Dean for the fatal shooting of a woman in her own home that occurred Saturday morning. Dean was released on $200,000 bond on Tuesday.Police bodycam video showed Dean approaching the door of the home of Atatiana Jefferson, 28, who was caring for her 8-year-old nephew early Saturday when he fired a single shot through the window, killing her.
