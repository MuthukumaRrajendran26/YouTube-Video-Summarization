After a weeks-long manhunt, RCMP announced Wednesday that Bryer Schmegelsky and Kam McLeod are believed to be dead. Manitoba RCMP said officers have located two male bodies, believed to belong to the suspects, near the shoreline of Nelson River. The discovery was made 10 a.m. Wednesday morning.Assistant Commissioner Jane MacLatchy said at a press conference that she is “confident” the bodies belong to the suspects, but cannot confirm until autopsy results are determined.
