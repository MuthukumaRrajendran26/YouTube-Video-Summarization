U.S. President Donald Trump on Thursday presented six-time NBA champion and Boston Celtics legend Bob Cousy with the Presidential Medal of Freedom, calling him a "true American original."The 91-year-old Cousy was known as "Mr. Basketball" during the 1950s and '60s, when he was a point guard for the Boston Celtics.Cousy won six league championships with the Celtics and the 1957 MVP title. The Bob Cousy award, which is given to the best point guard in men's college basketball, is named for him.
