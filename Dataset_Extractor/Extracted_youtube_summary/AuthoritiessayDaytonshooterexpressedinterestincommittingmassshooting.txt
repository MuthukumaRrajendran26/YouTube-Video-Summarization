The police and FBI provided an update on the Dayton mass shooting on Tuesday, August 6 as the latter said it's beginning to investigate "violent ideologies" the man police say committed the shooting showed to have sought out.Police also said that based on evidence gathered, the shooter had "expressed interest" in committing a mass shooting and were investigating with FBI how those ideologies he sought out may have influenced the shooting.Authorities said there is no evidence at this time that shows the shooting was racially motivated.
