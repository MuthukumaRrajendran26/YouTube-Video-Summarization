U.S. President Donald Trump met with Ukrainian President Volodymyr Zelenskiy in New York City on Wendesday.The meeting came under contentious circumstances after it was revealed that Trump had asked President Zelenskiy for information on former U.S. vice-president and Democratic foe Joe Biden during a call in July, a memorandum of a telephone call released by the White House on Wednesday says.In the call, Trump raised unsubstantiated allegations that Biden sought to interfere with a Ukrainian prosecutor’s investigation of his son Hunter.
