Teen Bianca Andreescu from Mississauga, Ont., beat tennis legend Serena Williams in the 2019 Rogers Cup, making her the first Canadian to win the tournament in 50 years.She is now ranked the 14th best player in the world. Andreescu also won at Indian Wells in March, the beginning of a 17-match win streak, not counting when she has had to retire from matches due to injury herself. She holds victories over seven of the top 10 players in the world this season, including Williams.
