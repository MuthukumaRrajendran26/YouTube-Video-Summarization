There are mixed reactions among the public after Don Cherry was fired from Hockey Night In Canada on Monday over comments he made in the Coach's Corner segment Saturday, where he suggested new immigrants don't wear poppies. Some people say it was the right choice to make, while others felt the move may have been more than necessary. Erica Vella reports.On social media, the reactions are also mixed with various hashtags being used pushing for Cherry's firing, while others saying they supported the long-time commentator.
