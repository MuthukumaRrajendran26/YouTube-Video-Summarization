On Monday Alykhan Velshi, vice president of corporate affairs at Huawei Canada, told reporters during an announcement in Ottawa that the company's investment in Canada's north is not a move intended to restore their tarnished image in Canada.Velshi also responded to allegations his parent company was building telecom infrastructure in North Korea,  blasted former cabinet minister John McCallum for his comments on Canada/China affairs, and joked with reporters that people often misconstrue Huawei offices to be "Dr. Evil's lair" where nefarious work is done.
