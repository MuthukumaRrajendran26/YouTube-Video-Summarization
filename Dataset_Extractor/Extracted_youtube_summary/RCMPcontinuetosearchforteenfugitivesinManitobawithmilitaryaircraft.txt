The RCMP continues their search for teen murder suspects, Kam McLeod and Bryer Schmegelsky, in Gillam, Man. The search continues on foot and in the air with the Hercules aircraft the Canadian military provided for the investigation. The fugitives are suspected to be involved in the murders of Lucas Fowler, Chynna Deese and Leonard Dyck. Global's Crystal Goomansingh has the updates.
