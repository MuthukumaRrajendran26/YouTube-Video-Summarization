The horrific shooting in Toronto's Danforth neighbourhood still continues to haunt victims, witnesses and the families of those who were killed or injured in the incident on July 22, 2018. In an attack that occurred over the span of 10 minutes and several blocks heading west from Logan Avenue on Danforth Avenue, two people were killed — 10-year-old Julianna Kozis and 18-year-old Reese Fallon — and 13 were injured before officials said the shooter, Faisal Hussain, fatally shot himself in the head.
