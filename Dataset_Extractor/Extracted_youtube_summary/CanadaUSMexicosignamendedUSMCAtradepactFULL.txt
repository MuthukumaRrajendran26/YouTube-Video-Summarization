Officials from Canada, the United States and Mexico signed an amended version of the new North American trade deal, called the U.S.-Mexico-Canada Agreement (CUSMA), Tuesday afternoon.The signing happened in Mexico City, with Deputy Prime Minister Chrystia Freeland representing Canada.Trump tweeted Tuesday morning that the revamped trade pact will “be great” for the United States.Prime Minister Justin Trudeau and Trump talked directly about the deal on Monday, Trudeau’s office reported, without providing further details.
