On Monday, U.S. President Donald Trump presented the Medal of Valor to six police officers who stopped the gunman in a mass shooting in Dayton, Ohio, in August. Nine people were killed and 27 more were injured, including the gunman's own sister in the mass shooting on Aug. 4, 2019. Five civilians also receive a commendation for their efforts to stop a shooter during a mass shooting at a Walmart in El Paso, Texas, in August.
