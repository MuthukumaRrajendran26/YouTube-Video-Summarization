**EDITOR’S NOTE: On Jan. 10, the Canadian government updated the number of Canadians killed in the Jan. 8 Ukraine International Airlines crash in Iran from 63 to 57.**Alberta Premier Jason Kenney addressed the media on Jan. 8 about the plane crash in Iran’s capital, Tehran, early Wednesday morning that killed all 176 people on board, including 63 Canadians – many of whom were Albertans. Kenney called the crash a “terrible day for Alberta.”  He also said that he supports Canadian efforts to "get to the bottom of the disaster."
