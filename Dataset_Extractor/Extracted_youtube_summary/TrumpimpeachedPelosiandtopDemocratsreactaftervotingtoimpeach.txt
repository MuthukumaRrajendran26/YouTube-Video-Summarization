U.S. House Speaker Nancy Pelosi, Judiciary Committee Chairman Jerrold Nadler, and Intelligence Committee Chairman Adam Schiff provided statements on Wednesday following the impeachment vote against President Donald Trump, with Pelosi stating that it was “a great day for the constitution of the United States.”The House Judiciary Committee voted on two impeachment articles against Trump: abuse of power and obstruction of Congress. The majority of the House voted to impeach the President on both articles. Trump has now become the third president in U.S. history to be impeached.
