There are now 72 confirmed or probable cases of COVID-19 in Manitoba, public health officials announced Sunday.That’s an increase of eight cases from Saturday to Sunday.Public health officials are now investigating how the new patients contracted the virus and are speaking to people they have been in contact with.One person is in intensive care battling the disease, and another is in hospital, said Dr. Brent Roussin, Manitoba’s chief provincial public health officer.One Manitoban, a woman in her 60s, died of the disease earlier this week.
