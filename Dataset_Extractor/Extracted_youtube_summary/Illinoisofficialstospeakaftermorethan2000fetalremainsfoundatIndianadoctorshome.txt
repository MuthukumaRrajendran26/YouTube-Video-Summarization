The Will County, Illinois sheriff, coroner and state's attorney discussed the more than 2,000 fetal remains found last week at the property of an Indiana abortion Dr. Ulrich Klopfer.They said in total 2,246 medically preserved remains were found. They were found after his family found some remains while searching his home following Klopfer's death. Investigators were brought in who then found more than 2,000 remains.
