RCMP have descended on York Landing, Man., as the hunt for two suspects wanted in connection with three murders in northern B.C. continues. Bryer Schmegelsky, 18, and Kam McLeod, 19, are suspects in the deaths of three people in northern B.C. earlier this month. They remain at large despite a six-day manhunt and more than 200 tips from the public, RCMP said Sunday.Investigators have been scouring a remote area of northern Manitoba as part of a nationwide search for the pair. The last confirmed sighting was in Gillam, Man., on Monday.York Landing is about 200 km west of Gillam.Sean O’Shea has the latest.
