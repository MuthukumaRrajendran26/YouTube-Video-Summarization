A woman in her 20s has been confirmed by Canadian health officials as the third case of the new coronavirus in Ontario on Friday, making her the fourth in Canada.The patient returned from Wuhan, China — which is said to be the epicentre of the outbreak — to Toronto and took a “private vehicle” to London, Ont.  Officials said the woman was asymptomatic when she arrived on Jan. 23, however, they said she began to exhibit symptoms of the virus the next day and went to the hospital.
