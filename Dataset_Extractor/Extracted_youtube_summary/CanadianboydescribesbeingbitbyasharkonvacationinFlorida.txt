11-year-old Christian Mariani was vacationing in Fort Lauderdale, Florida with his family when he was bitten by what medical experts believe may have been a small shark.Mariani said he was in knee high water when he felt something bite his right foot. Broward Health Medical Center's Dr. Francis Amador told reporters the bite marks were small and in the shape of a a semi circle, which he said was likely the bite of a small shark.Mariani received 17 stitches and is expected to make a full recovery.
