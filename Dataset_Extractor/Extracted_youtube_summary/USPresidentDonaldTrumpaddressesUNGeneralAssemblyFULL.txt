U.S President Donald Trump addressed the UN General Assembly on Tuesday where he denounced Iran's "blood lust," and "open border activists" as he called illegal immigration one of the world's must crucial challenges.Trump also had a stern message for China and its president, Xi Jinping, in what was his third annual appearance at the U.N. saying the world is watching how Beijing handles mass demonstrations in Hong Kong that has raised concerns about a potential Chinese crackdown.Trump also called on nations around the globe to embrace nationalism and reject globalism, saying wise leaders put their own people and countries first.
