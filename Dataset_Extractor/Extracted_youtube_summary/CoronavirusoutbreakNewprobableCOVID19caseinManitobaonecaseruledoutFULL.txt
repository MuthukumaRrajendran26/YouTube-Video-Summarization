Manitoba's chief public health officer Dr. Brent Roussin and Lanette Siragusa, chief nursing officer for Manitoba Shared Health held the daily coronavirus briefing on Monday.Dr. Roussin announced a new travel-related probable case of COVID-19 for a man in his 50s in Winnipeg. He also said a previous probable case was ruled out after it tested negative at the national lab. The total number of confirmed and probable cases in Manitoba remains at 20. Dr. Roussin stressed on Manitobans to avoid all non-essential travel outside the province and to practice social distancing.
