Canadian Prime Minister Justin Trudeau met face-to-face with U.S. President Donald Trump Sunday on the sidelines of the G7 summit in Biarritz, France, where the two were expected to discuss trade and economic issues amid rising concerns about a possible global recession.Speaking to reporters before beginning their bilateral chat the two G7 leaders talked about their shared commitment to seeing the new North American trade deal enacted, dubbed the U.S. Mexico Canada Trade Agreement, or USMCA.The Trudeau government has been waiting to see how the trade deal is dealt with by Washington before introducing it in Parliament.
