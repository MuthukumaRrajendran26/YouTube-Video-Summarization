The two Port Alberni teens first thought to be missing, are now considered suspects in a double homicide investigation in northern B.C. and into the death of a man whose body was found near their burned out vehicle.  Chynna Deese and Lucas Fowler were found shot dead on Highway 97 on July 15. Kam McLeod, 19, and Bryer Schmegelsky, 18, are also wanted in connection with the death of a man found about two kilometres from their burning pickup truck on Highway 37 last Friday, the BC RCMP said Tuesday.Rumina Daya has the latest.
