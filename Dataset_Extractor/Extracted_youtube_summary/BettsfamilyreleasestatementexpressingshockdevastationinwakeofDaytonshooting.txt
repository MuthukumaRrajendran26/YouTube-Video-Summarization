Bellbrook police released a statement on behalf of the Betts family on Tuesday, August 6. They said they were "shocked and devastated" by the incident in the Oregon District and send their prayers and condolences to the victims, their families and friends. In the statement, the family said they were cooperating with authorities in the investigation, but also ask for privacy as they mourn the loss of their son and daughter. The family's son Connor is the man police say committed the mass shooting in Dayton on Sunday morning. The family also lost their daughter, Megan, who was killed during the shooting.
