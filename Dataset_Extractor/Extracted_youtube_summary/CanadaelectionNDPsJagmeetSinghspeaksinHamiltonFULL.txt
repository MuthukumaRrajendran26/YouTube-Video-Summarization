New Democrats Party Leader Jagmeet Singh spoke in Hamilton, Ont. on Thursday, Sept. 19, 2019 and announced if elected they would help small businesses and workers with dental coverage and pharmacare.  He also spoke about Liberal Leader Justin Trudeau's apology for recently-surfaced examples of racist makeup and said that it would be up to Canadians to decide if the apology "is enough" and that he would focus on helping those who are feeling an impact after the examples surfaced.
