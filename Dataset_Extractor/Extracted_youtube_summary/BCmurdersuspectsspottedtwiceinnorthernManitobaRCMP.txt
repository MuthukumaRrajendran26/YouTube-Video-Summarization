RCMP say there have been additional sightings of two young men wanted in the deaths of three people in northern B.C.The police force said Thursday that Bryer Schmegelsky, 18, and Kam McLeod, 19, were seen twice in the Gillam, Man., area.The sightings took place before a torched RAV 4 was discovered in the northern Manitoba community earlier this week.
