New York Governor Andrew Cuomo called on the U.S. government for more supplies and resources as the number of new cases of COVID-19 in the state jumped to 4800 in a single day.  So far, 61,000 have been tested for the novel coronavirus disease in New York.During Sunday's briefing, Cuomo requested the help of the Army Corps to erect temporary hospitals in the hardest-hit zones and called on the Trump administration for more supplies to help prevent the spread of the novel coronavirus. Cuomo also said that the state has secured supplies of Hydroxychloroquine, Zithromax and Chloroquine and the trial drug will be implemented on Tuesday.
