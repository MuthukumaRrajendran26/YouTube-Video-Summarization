Democratic presidential candidate Joe Biden was joined by former Democratic presidential hopefuls Amy Klobuchar and Beto O'Rourke at an event in Dallas, Texas, on Monday night. Klobuchar and O'Rourke formally endorsed Biden at the event, continuing a strong week for the former Vice-President of the United States, fresh off his commanding win in the South Carolina primary.The rally comes on the eve of Super Tuesday, when the greatest number of states hold primary elections
