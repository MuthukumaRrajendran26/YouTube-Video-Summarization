The town of Gillam, Man. is attempting to return to a sense of normalcy after RCMP said the manhunt for two suspects wanted in connection to three murders in B.C. had ended. It came when police said they had discovered two bodies they believe to be that of the suspects, Kam Mcleod and Bryer Schmegelsky. Residents say people are relieved and feel like they can go about their days without fear. In the wake of news of the manhunt's end, the cousin of Canadian victim Leonard Dyck spoke with Global News about his thoughts knowing it may be over.
