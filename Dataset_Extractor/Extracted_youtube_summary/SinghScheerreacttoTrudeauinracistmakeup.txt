NDP Leader Jagmeet Singh and Conservative Leader Andrew Scheer reacted to the images and video of Liberal Leader Justin Trudeau dressed up in brownface and blackface on Wednesday and Thursday. As Abigail Bimman reports, Singh is focused on how these images will affect people of colour, especially children. And for Scheer, Mike Le Couteur reports the Conservative leader says Trudeau has lost the moral authority to govern the country.
