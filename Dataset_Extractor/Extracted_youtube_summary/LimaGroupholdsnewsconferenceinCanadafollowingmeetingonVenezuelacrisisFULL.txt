Partners of the Lima Group held a news conference following a meeting hosted by Canada on Thursday in an attempt to build new momentum in its effort to bring democracy to Venezuela.Canada and dozens of countries recognize Guaido as Venezuela’s legitimate leader, and view Maduro as a dictator who stole his country’s last election.Canada last hosted the Lima Group one year ago in Ottawa.The group includes Argentina, Brazil, Canada, Chile, Colombia, Costa Rica, Guatemala, Honduras, Paraguay, Peru and Venezuela.
