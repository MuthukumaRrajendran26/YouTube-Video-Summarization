U.S. President Donald Trump called NBA Golden State Warriors coach Steve Kerr a "little boy" who didn't want to say anything bad about China when questioned about Chinese pressure on the NBA, in a growing dispute over a tweet by a team official supporting the recent protests in Hong Kong.Trump also criticized Gregg Popovich, coach of the San Antonio Spurs, for a similar reaction in an interview about China, despite him not being "quite as scared" as Kerr.
