Pedro Pierluisi took questions after being tapped to become governor of Puerto Rico following the resignation of Ricardo Rossello.Pierluisi was picked by Rossello originally to become Secretary of State, but following the announcement the embattled governor would resign and Justice Minister Wanda Vazquez Garced saying she did not wish to take the position of governor, the role would fall on Pierluisi once confirmed. On Friday, however, after Rossello officially resigned, Pierluisi was confirmed by the state House of Representatives and the governor's office said he had been sworn in. The state Senate however has called the move "illegal" as they have yet to confirm him.
