Mexico's foreign minister, Marcelo Ebrard, said Monday that at least eight Mexican nationals had been killed in the mass shooting in Texas on Saturday, while at least six were still injured. He also called the shooting an "act of terror" against Mexican citizens in a U.S. territory.Ebrard said they were working with U.S. federal, state and local authorities in the investigation and are looking into the possibility of extraditing the suspect in the shooting.
