U.S. Democratic presidential candidate and former vice-president,Joe Biden spoke to reporters on Wednesday, urging his supporters against resorting to “negative attacks” that would help Donald Trump.Biden, the former vice-president whose campaign was viewed as having hit a skid just a few weeks ago, won nine of 14 states voting on Super Tuesday, including surprise wins in Texas and Massachusetts.Mike Bloomberg also announced today that he will be suspending his campaign and endorsing Biden.
