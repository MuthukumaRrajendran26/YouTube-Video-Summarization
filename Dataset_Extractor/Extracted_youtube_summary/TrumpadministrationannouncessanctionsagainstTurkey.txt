The Trump administration announced sanctions on Monday against Turkey as the country  continues its ongoing incursion into Syria. Treasury Secretary Steve Mnuchin and Vice-President Mike Pence spoke Monday about how Turkey would be able to see newly imposed sanctions removed, saying what has been put in place would harm the Turkish economy severely. Asked about recent discussions Trump had with Turkish President Tayyip Erdogan, Pence said he did "not give the green light" for Turkey to invade Syria.
