Global National's Dawna Friesen talks with independent candidate Jody Wilson-Raybould about her time in the Trudeau government, what it's like to run as an independent and how important the issue of trust is to Canadians in the upcoming election.Wilson-Raybould won the riding of Vancouver Granville as a star Liberal candidate in 2015 with 44 per cent of the vote.This time, she is up against Liberal candidate Taleeb Noormohamed, a tech entrepreneur, NDP candidate Yvonne Hanson, a climate activist, and Conservative Zach Segal, a former political staffer in Ottawa. The Green candidate is Louise Boutin, a realtor.
