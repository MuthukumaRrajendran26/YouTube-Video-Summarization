At the corner of Greenwood Ave. and Gerrard St. E. in Toronto is a restaurant that celebrates Indigenous cuisine. This week on ‘Fresh Off The Plate,’ Global News’ Liem Vu visits Tea-N-Bannock and meets the people behind the café and what it means to them.On the menu is a beautiful selection of Canadian-sourced meats and fish, including the Arctic char-grilled dinner and the bison burger. Each dish is presented simply and highlights the fresh ingredients.
