U.S. President Donald Trump has now been officially impeached.In a historic move on Wednesday evening, lawmakers in the House of Representatives voted to impeach U.S. President Donald Trump. Trump is now only the third U.S. president to have been impeached under the U.S. Constitution.Now that he has been impeached, the process moves on to the Senate, which is tasked with deciding whether to remove the president from office.Jackson Proskow explains what happens next.
