U.S. President Donald Trump spoke about multiple issues on Thursday as he headed to a rally in Ohio, including new tariffs he had issued against China which will see 10% levied on $300 billion worth of Chinese imports.He also weighed in on the Democratic debates, giving his opinion on the performances of Elizabeth Warren, Kamala Harris and former vice-president Joe Biden. He also said he is considering a blockade or quarantine of Venezuela given the amount of involvement China and Iran have in the country.
