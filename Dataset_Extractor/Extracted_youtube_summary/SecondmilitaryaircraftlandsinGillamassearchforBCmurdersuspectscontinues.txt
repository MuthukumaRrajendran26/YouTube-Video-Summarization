Bryer Schmegelsky and Kam McLeod remain at large despite a five-day manhunt and more than 200 tips from the public, Manitoba RCMP said Sunday.The RCMP are scouring a remote area of northern Manitoba as part of a nationwide search for the pair, who are suspects in the deaths of three people in northern B.C. earlier this month.Schmegelsky, 18, and McLeod, 19, were last sighted in the town of Gillam, Man., on Monday.Over the weekend, investigators were canvassing homes and searching buildings in Gillam and neighbouring Fox Lake Cree Nation.As of Sunday afternoon, RCMP said that 250 homes were visited and canvassing of Fox Lake had been completed.
