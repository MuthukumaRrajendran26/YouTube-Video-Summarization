Speaking in Kingsclear, NB on Tuesday, Conservative Party Leader Andrew Scheer proposed his party's plan to reduce the number of service hours required to qualify for federal tax credits from 200 to 150 hours per year for volunteer firefighters and search and rescue volunteers.When asked about re-opening the abortion debate, Scheer told reporters that he was "personally pro-life," but pointed to all his policies in his platform that he says proves he and his party supports the rights of women, and that as Prime Minister he would ensure their rights are "always protected."
