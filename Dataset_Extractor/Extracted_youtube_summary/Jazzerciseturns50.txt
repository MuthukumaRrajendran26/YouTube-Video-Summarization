Nothing screams the 1980s quite like Jazzercise — the fitness program features some of the most iconic elements of the decade, from the perms to the nylons.But, what has become one of the biggest fitness franchises ever was actually founded decades before this: in 1969.This year, the franchise turns 50 years old, and the company has boogied way past the era when it gained its fame and has morphed into a modern global empire that continues to keep people around the world moving.
