Conservative cabinet minister John Baird has been tapped to lead an external review of what went wrong and what went right during the latest election campaign, said one MP who spoke on condition of anonymity.The party’s 121 members of Parliament were meeting today in Ottawa for their first formal caucus meeting since the Oct. 21 vote.Among their first orders of business was to decide whether to adopt a rule that would allow 20 per cent of their group to trigger a leadership review.Many MPs said they wanted the question of Scheer’s future to be decided by the entire party.
